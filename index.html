About Huskies

Huskies are a very stubborn yet smart breed. They are amazing friendly pets but have some downsides, in a manner of speaking:

  • They are very active so they require ALOT of exercise.
  • Huskies are also very vocal, so prepare to have a song ready for them to sing and howl along to.
  • They shed ALOT of hair.
